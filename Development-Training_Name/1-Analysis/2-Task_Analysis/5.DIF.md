## Legend:
- **Difficulty:** 
  - V = Very Difficult
  - M = Moderately Difficult
  - N = Not Difficult
- **Importance:** 
  - Y = Yes
  - N = No
- **Frequency:** 
  - V = Very Frequent
  - M = Moderately Frequent
  - I = Infrequent
- **Training Priority:**
  - T = Train
  - OT = Over Train
  - NFT = No Formal Training
